Meet The Team

For over 27 years, Energy Services and Products Corporation (ESPC)has benefited
from an amazing group of individuals steering our course. Our executive team
has a proven history of utilizing their considerable knowledge and experience
to provide our clients with quality services andvalued engineered solutions.
Each team member shares the same commitment to serve our clients with a
deliverable that always meets and often exceeds their highest expectations.

Tony Padilla

CEO

Tony founded ESPC in 1994. Tony is responsible for setting the strategic priorities for ESPC, as well asproviding executive
direction for the company's diverseportfolio. Prior to establishing
ESPC, Tony worked for 18 years at Florida Power Corporation
(now Duke Energy) as a mechanical engineer and manager of R&D for
the new technology department.Tony also served as a Principal
for the clean energy and vehicle program at the University
of South Florida (USF).

Melissa Padilla

President

Melissa is responsible for the company's overall growth and financial
health as well as the company's day-to-day operations, including the
acquisition and execution of work, and business functions. Prior to
joining ESPC, Melissa worked as a Director in Finance at the Pew Charitable Trusts in Washington, DC where she provided innovative solutions and
delivered breakthrough results in the contracts,grants and procurement
departments. Melissa earned a bachelor's degree from the University of
Florida and her doctorate in law from the Columbus School of Law at Catholic University. She is a member of the bar in Florida.

Bryan Barlett

Vice President, Design & Permitting

Bryan is responsible for the design and permitting divisions.
Bryan Joined ESPC as a drafter in 1998 and has gained invaluable
experience in all facets of the GIS and telecommunications industry.
In his 21 years with ESPC, Bryan’s drive, ingenuity, and performance
propelled him to the top of the design and permitting division.
Under his leadership, the division continues to grow, while still
offering the same hands on relationship to its clients. Bryan earned
a bachelor of science degree in computer science with an emphasis on
software development and information systems from the University of South Florida. ​

Ron Alvarez

Director, Permitting

Ron is responsible for the permitting division. Ron
